## 🛍️ Shopify Integration (If You Have a Store)

### What AUREM Does:
1. **Tracks Inventory in Real-Time**
   - Syncs with your Shopify store every second
   - Knows exactly what's in stock

2. **Never Over-Promises**
   - If Rose-Gen sells out, AI knows instantly
   - Says: "Just sold the last one! Want me to notify you when back?"

3. **Smart Suggestions**
   - AI suggests alternatives if item out of stock
   - Prevents lost sales

### Setup (One-Time, 5 Minutes):

**In Shopify Admin**:
1. Go to: Settings → Notifications → Webhooks
2. Click "Create webhook"
3. Event: `Order creation`
4. URL: `https://[your-domain]/api/shopify/webhooks/orders/create`
5. Format: JSON
6. Click "Save"

Repeat for:
- Event: `Product update`
- URL: `https://[your-domain]/api/shopify/webhooks/products/update`

**That's it!** AUREM now knows your inventory in real-time.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I'm not getting email notifications"
1. Check spam folder
2. Verify email address in account settings
3. Contact support if still not receiving

### "AI isn't capturing leads"
1. Make sure customers are actually expressing buying intent
2. Keywords: "buy", "book", "interested", "price", "appointment"
3. AI needs contact info in conversation (name, phone, or email)

### "Dashboard shows zero leads"
1. Give it time—AI needs real customer conversations
2. Test with: Send yourself a message like "I want to book"
3. Check that you're logged into correct account

### "Shopify inventory not syncing"
1. Verify webhooks are set up in Shopify Admin
2. Check webhook delivery logs in Shopify
3. Contact support with your store URL
