AUREM Economic Intelligence Module
Compliance Statement — Polaris Built Inc.

AUREM aggregates publicly available 
economic data from official government 
sources including the Bank of Canada 
Valet API and other public data providers.
All data is presented for general business 
context purposes only. AUREM does not 
provide investment advice, securities 
recommendations, or trading signals.
AUREM is not registered as an investment 
advisor, portfolio manager, or dealer 
under the Securities Act (Ontario) or 
any other securities legislation.
Users should consult a registered 
financial professional before making 
any investment decisions.
